Sportsman

The Sportsman team produces a 12 lb autonomous low-KE robot. Sportsman aims to to creatively develop a new, interdisciplinary robot that bridges the gap between computer science and mechanical engineering. This team specializes in Fusion 360, the robotic design process, and interdisciplinary collaboration (with Sportsman Firmware). Apply if you're interested in creative combat robot design, manufacturing, and working on a large multidisciplinary team.

Firmware

The Firmware team programs, wires & tests our 12 lb autonomous low-KE robot. Firmware is crucial in detecting boundaries & finding / attacking other robots. This team specializes in C++, microcontrollers, sensors, circuit design, interdisciplinary collaboration (with Sportsman), and kinematics. Apply if you're interested in the fundamental concepts of robot programming and electronics (circuit design, low-level programming, etc.), fusing software and hardware, and applications of your Cornell course knowledge.

Business and Media

The Business and Media team manages the team's image as well as raises funds to build the robots. This includes hosting events like a Krispy Kreme fundraiser, merch design & distribution, and working with Cornell fundraising campaigns. This team specializes in talking to companies, organizing team events, and managing and coding our website. Apply if you're interested in promoting / marketing events, graphic design, and forming relationships with our sponsors.
